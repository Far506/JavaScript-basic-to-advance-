Born in Rosario, Argentina on June 24, 1987, Messi showed a talent for football from a young age. He joined Newell's Old Boys, his local team, in 1995. At age 13, he moved to Barcelona, Spain to join FC Barcelona's under-14 team.

Considered one of the greatest soccer players of all time, Ronaldo holds many records, including most goals in the UEFA Champions League, most international goals, and most appearances in a national team. He's also won five Ballon d'Or awards, the most for a European player.
